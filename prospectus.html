Affiliation (CBSE)

Six Schools are affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi

Medium of Instruction : Education is imparted through English Medium in all Schools.

Courses of Studies

  • Pre-Primary to class X- (as per CBSE syllabus) English, Hindi, Sanskrit, Maths, Science, Social Science, General Knowledge, Physical Education, Art Education, Work Experience, Socially Useful Productive Work as compulsory and Foundation of Information Technology as an additional subject in classes IX-X

    NOTE

    1. Computer Education starts from class.
    2. Sanskrit teaching starts from class VI.
    3. Social Science is an integrated course of History, Geography, Civics, Economics & Disaster Management.
